What We Do

Walking In The Sun Rescue, a 501(c) (3) charitable organization, is a foster based rescue. Our focus is to rescue the homeless, treat the ill and injured, and to relieve the suffering of companion animals in Morehouse Parish, Louisiana.

  • Rescue & Transport: Same-day pulls and volunteer rides to safety.
  • Medical Care: Vaccinations, spay/neuter, and specialized treatment.
  • Foster Network: Short-term homes—we supply food and vet care, you supply the love.
  • Adoption Matchmaking: Home checks, lifetime support, and a no-questions return policy.
  • Community Outreach: Low-cost clinics and humane-care education.
  • Emergency Response: 24-hour deployment during disasters or cruelty cases.
